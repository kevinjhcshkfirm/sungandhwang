PAUL W. KIM

Paul W. Kim, JD, MPH, assists clients in the health care industry on all aspects of health law, except FDA compliance and medical malpractice defense. From CMS, OIG, and OCR regulatory compliance to government investigations, Paul helps providers, medical device manufacturers, and clinical researchers with the following legal and operational issues:

  • Medicare Coverage, Coding, and Reimbursement
  • Audits and Appeals
  • Enrollment Suspensions and Revocations
  • Whistleblower Actions
  • HIPAA and Cyberattacks

Paul also serves as the firm's Chief Privacy Officer.
